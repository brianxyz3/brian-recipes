Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 Large onion, sliced
  • 1 Tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 Cans of tomato paste
  • Stewed tomatoes
  • Cayenne/Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Chopped fresh rosemary
  • Uncooked white rice
  • Diced carrots
  • Ground nutmeg
  • 4 Pieces of chicken

Steps

  1. Pour oil into large saucepan. Cook onion in oil over medium-low heat until translucent. Soak rice in boiled water.
  2. Stir in stewed tomatooes and tomato paste and season with salt, black pepper, Cayenne pepper, Worcestershire sauce and rosemary. Cover and allow to cook till boil. Reduce the heat, stir and add chicken pieces. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Stir in rice, carrot and season with nutmeg. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer till chicken is fork-tender and the rice is cooked.
