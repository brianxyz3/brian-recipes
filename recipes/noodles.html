Noodles

Ingredients

  • 2 Pack of instant noodle
  • 1 Tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 Egg
  • Sliced tomato
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Black pepper
  • Diced onion
  • Diced carrot
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook noodle in water for 5 minutes, then filter.
  2. Add olive oil to saucepan with low heat. Fry egg and keep. Add sliced tomato, diced onion, dice carrot, cayenne pepper, black pepper, salt. stir then allow to simmer.
  3. Add noodles to stir fry, add instant noodle spice and simmer. Serve with fried egg.
