Egusi Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup pumpkin seeds
  • Cubed beef stew meat
  • Salt
  • Small onion, chopped
  • Habanero peppers, seeded and minced
  • Fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh spinach, washed and chopped
  • Tomato paste

Steps

  1. Blend pumpkin seed in a blender until powdery, set aside.
  2. Cut beef into cubes , season with salt.
  3. heat oil in large pot over medium-high heat. Cook beef in hot oil until brown but not cooked through.
  4. Blend tomatoes, onion, and peppers in a blender, until smooth. Stir tomato mixture into beef, reduce heat to medium-low and cover. Cook until meat is tender.
  5. Add shrimp, tomato sauce, water, and tomato paste, simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Stir in spinach and powdered pumkin seeds. Continue simmering for 10 more minutes.
